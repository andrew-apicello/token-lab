MyShare (MYS) is a reward token users earn by sharing sponsored content on social media platforms.

How It Works

Advertisers

Build ads by linking or uploading content and choose reach and rate.

MyShare

Reviews content, verifies payment, approves ads, and distributes rewards.

Members

Earn MYS tokens by sharing posts on various social media platforms.

Value Proposition for Marketers

Name Your Price

Allowing the market to price a "share" gives marketers a low price point for promoting their media.

P2P Advertising

Millenials are both skeptical of traditional media, and value the advice of their colleagues. MyShare allows marketers to directly empower promoters of their brand.

User Excitement

Millenials are excited by crypto assets. MyShare enables them to earn MYS tokens risk free for behavior they already exhibit, bringing an authentic and cutting edge face to your brand.

Partnership

MyShare is committed to working with established social media platforms and adding to the impressive ecosystem they have succesfully built.

Advertise with Us

Word-of-Mouth Marketing

Research has shown that peer-to-peer promoting can be more successful than traditional advertising.

Name Your Price Ads

Advertisers will be able to compete on our dynamic marketplace to determine the lowest price per share; ensuring members are provided with offers of the highest quality.

Transparent Results

Ad fraud is costing advertisers an estimated $6.5 billion a year. MyShare will provide a more transparent system by utilizing blockchain technology to eliminate ad fraud.

Demographic Targeting

Launch effective, targeted campaigns with the ability to customize your audience by location, age, interests, and more.

Connect to Millennials

Traditional marketing strategies are less effective when reaching millennials. We believe we can spark interest in quality content and use Shared Media to solidify this new communication channel.

Free Clicks and Views

With MyShare, any organic, word-of-mouth campaign traction the advertisement acquires post-share, will be of sole benefit to the advertiser (i.e. clicks, shares, likes, etc) at no extra cost.
